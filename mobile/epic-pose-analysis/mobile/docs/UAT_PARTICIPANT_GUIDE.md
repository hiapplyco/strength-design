## What is Pose Analysis?

Pose analysis uses AI to evaluate your exercise form in real-time. Simply record or upload a video of yourself performing an exercise (squat, deadlift, or pushup), and you'll receive:

- **Form Score** (0-100): Overall technique rating
- **Actionable Feedback**: Specific improvements to make
- **Visual Overlay**: See your form highlighted on video
- **Progress Tracking**: Watch your form improve over time

## Tips for Effective Testing

### Do's ‚úÖ
- **Be honest**: We want real feedback, not sugar-coated
- **Be specific**: "The button was hard to find" is more helpful than "bad UX"
- **Use your own device**: Test in real-world conditions
- **Test at different times**: Morning, evening, at gym, at home
- **Take screenshots**: Capture bugs or confusing moments
- **Ask questions**: Use the Slack/Discord channel

### Don'ts ‚ùå
- Don't rush through scenarios - take your time
- Don't skip feedback forms - they're crucial
- Don't test only perfect scenarios - try to break things
- Don't worry about "looking bad" on camera - this is about form analysis, not appearance

### Recording Tips üìπ
- Ensure good lighting (face a window or light source)
- Show your full body in frame (head to toe)
- Use a phone stand or prop against a wall
- Record from the side for squat/deadlift
- Record from above for pushups (place phone on floor)

## Reporting Bugs

If you encounter a bug or crash:

1. **Note the details**:
   - What were you doing when it happened?
   - What did you expect to happen?
   - What actually happened?

2. **Take a screenshot** (if possible)

3. **Report in Slack/Discord** using this template:
   ```
   üêõ BUG REPORT
   Device: [iPhone 13, Android Galaxy S21, etc.]
   Scenario: [What were you testing?]
   Steps to reproduce:
   1. First I did...
   2. Then I...
   3. Then it crashed/froze/etc.

   Expected: [What should have happened]
   Actual: [What actually happened]

   Screenshot: [Attach if available]
   ```

4. **Severity levels**:
   - üî¥ **Critical**: Can't use feature at all, app crashes
   - üü° **High**: Major annoyance, workflow broken
   - üü¢ **Medium**: Minor issue, has workaround
   - üîµ **Low**: Cosmetic issue, typo, polish

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: How much time should I spend on this?**
A: 2-3 hours total over 7 days. Take breaks - don't do it all at once.

**Q: Do I need to complete all scenarios?**
A: We'd love you to complete all 10, but if you can only do 5-7, that's still valuable.

**Q: What if I don't know how to do these exercises?**
A: That's okay! We provide sample videos you can upload. Just test the app functionality.

**Q: Can I test with a friend or trainer?**
A: Absolutely! Having someone help you film is realistic and encouraged.

**Q: What if I find the feature confusing or frustrating?**
A: Perfect! That's exactly what we need to know. Be honest in your feedback.

**Q: Will my videos be shared publicly?**
A: No. Videos are only used for analysis and stored securely. We won't share them without your explicit permission.

**Q: Can I suggest new features?**
A: Yes! We have a section in the final survey for feature requests.

**Q: What happens after testing ends?**
A: We'll send a summary of findings and the improvements we're making based on your feedback. You'll get early access to the updated version.
